Resource Architecture

Soil, irrigation, livestock, machinery & agricultural resources framework

A modular, scalable knowledge system that connects soil profiles, irrigation methods, livestock systems, farm machinery, fertilizers, plant protection, glossary, and calculators into one integrated ecosystem.

Soil knowledge framework

Reusable soil profiles that connect to crops, articles, and calculators.

  • Unique ID for stable references
  • Soil name and local names for regional discoverability
  • General description and key characteristics
  • Texture, drainage, water holding capacity, and organic matter overview
  • General management notes and improvement strategies
  • Links to related crops, articles, calculators, and glossary terms

Irrigation framework

Reusable irrigation method profiles.

Irrigation methods supported

  • Surface irrigation
  • Sprinkler irrigation
  • Drip irrigation
  • Rain-fed farming
  • Future irrigation methods

Each profile includes

  • Overview and general principles
  • Advantages and limitations
  • Suitable applications and crops
  • Water conservation concepts
  • Links to related articles and calculators

Weather and climate knowledge model

Support educational content about farming considerations.

  • Temperature effects and crop implications
  • Rainfall patterns and water management
  • Humidity and disease prevention
  • Wind effects and risk management
  • Seasonal climate patterns for Pakistan
  • Weather risks and farmer preparedness
  • General farming considerations by season
  • Future weather API integration fields

Fertilizer framework

Reusable fertilizer and nutrient records.

  • Product name and nutrient summary
  • General purpose and use categories
  • Storage guidance and handling precautions
  • Safety notes and best practices
  • Links to related crops, articles, and calculators

Plant protection framework

Separate profiles for pests, diseases, and weeds.

  • Name and affected crops
  • General symptoms and identification guidance
  • General prevention concepts
  • When professional advice may be appropriate
  • Links to related articles and glossary terms

Livestock framework

Reusable livestock and animal husbandry profiles.

Animal systems supported

  • Cattle and buffalo
  • Goats and sheep
  • Poultry
  • Fish farming
  • Beekeeping

Each profile includes

  • Overview and production type
  • General care topics and housing considerations
  • Nutrition and health topics
  • Related articles and guides
  • Future calculators for breeding, feed, and cost

Farm machinery framework

Reusable machinery and equipment profiles.

Equipment categories

  • Tractors
  • Harvesters
  • Seed drills
  • Sprayers
  • Laser land levelers
  • Solar pumps
  • Other farm equipment

Each entry includes

  • Overview and typical applications
  • Safety considerations and best practices
  • Maintenance overview and care guidance
  • Links to related guides and reference material

Agricultural glossary framework

Reusable glossary entries for agriculture terminology.

  • Term and scientific name
  • Definition and simple explanation
  • Category for organization
  • Synonyms and related terms
  • Links to related articles and calculators

Unit conversion framework

Reusable conversion support for farming calculations.

Conversion categories

  • Area (acres, hectares, marlas, kanals)
  • Weight (kilograms, pounds, maunds)
  • Volume (liters, gallons, cubic meters)
  • Length (meters, feet, miles)
  • Temperature (Celsius, Fahrenheit)
  • Currency support (future-ready)

Each conversion defines

  • Input and output units
  • Conversion formulas and accuracy rules
  • Validation rules for user input

Cross-linking and search architecture

Avoiding orphan content by connecting every knowledge item.

  • Every knowledge object connects to related categories, articles, calculators, glossary terms, and FAQs
  • Searchable fields include name, keywords, synonyms, category, description, and language identifier
  • Each record includes created date, updated date, content version, editorial status, and review frequency

Multilingual and future extensibility

The architecture supports growth without redesign.

Language support

  • English as the primary language
  • Urdu, Punjabi, Saraiki, and additional languages in the future
  • Language-independent IDs for stable references across translations

Future modules ready to add

  • Market prices and commodity tracking
  • Weather API integration
  • Government notifications and policy updates
  • Satellite data and remote sensing
  • AI farming assistant
  • GIS mapping and location services
