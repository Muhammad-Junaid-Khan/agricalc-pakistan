Rice Irrigation Planning for Higher Productivity

Smart scheduling and water depth control help rice growers reduce waste and improve yields.

Introduction

Rice farming requires careful water management, especially during seedling establishment and grain filling. Maintain a consistent schedule and check soil moisture so the crop gets enough water without over-irrigation. Combine your plan with fertilizer timing and field leveling for the best return.
