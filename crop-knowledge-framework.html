Crop Data Model

Reusable crop knowledge framework for educational articles, calculators, FAQs, and future multilingual growth

The framework supports a consistent crop record structure while keeping recommendations flexible and easy to update over time.

Supported crop groups

The structure is designed to support major crops now and additional crops later.

Wheat

Crop profile, seasonality, growth stages, and common care topics.

Rice

Irrigation, land preparation, growth overview, and harvest-related content.

Cotton

Plant protection, nutrition, and crop management references.

Maize

Growth stages, nutrition, planting, and problem-solving support.

Sugarcane

Growth cycle, management, harvest overview, and related resources.

Pulses, oilseeds, vegetables, fruits, fodder

Expandable categories for broader crop knowledge coverage.

Core crop profile

Each crop record should be a reusable building block for content and tools.

  • Unique ID for stable references.
  • Common name for primary display.
  • Scientific name for taxonomy and reference.
  • Local names for regional usage and discoverability.
  • Crop category for grouping and navigation.
  • Short description for overview content.
  • Featured image and related media assets.
  • Related categories for linking the crop to relevant hubs.

Growth and cultivation framework

The crop model should support a structured educational path from basics to detailed practice.

Growth information

  • Growing season
  • Climate requirements
  • General soil suitability
  • Growth stages
  • Typical crop duration
  • Harvest stage

Cultivation sections

  • Land preparation
  • Sowing
  • General crop care
  • Irrigation overview
  • Plant nutrition overview
  • Harvest
  • Post-harvest handling
  • Storage overview

Each section should link to dedicated educational articles rather than storing detailed instructions directly inside the profile.

Problem management and support content

Crop profiles should connect to practical problem-solving content.

  • Common pests
  • Common diseases
  • Common weeds
  • Nutrient deficiency topics
  • General prevention practices

Each item should link to dedicated educational pages that can be refreshed independently.

Calculator and FAQ integration

Crop profiles should act as connectors between education and practical decision tools.

Calculator references

  • Profit calculator
  • Seed calculator
  • Fertilizer calculator
  • Water calculator
  • Area converter
  • Yield calculator (future)

FAQ integration

  • Frequently asked questions
  • Related answers
  • Links to detailed guides

Media, glossary, and internal linking

The crop profile should be a hub for discovery, not just a static record.

  • Featured image, gallery, illustrations, diagrams, and future video support
  • Descriptive metadata for every media item
  • Automatic links to relevant glossary terms and scientific terminology
  • Links to the category hub, related crops, articles, calculators, FAQs, and glossary entries

Versioning, search, and multilingual readiness

The model should remain maintainable as content grows.

  • Created date, updated date, content version, editorial status, and review schedule
  • Search support by common name, scientific name, local name, category, keywords, and synonyms
  • Language-independent identifiers and localized titles and descriptions for future translations
  • Room for future additions such as recommended varieties, regional notes, market information, weather integration, and research references
